The Dark Times: From the hidden secrets of history, to unusual details of our daily lives, to the darkest corners of the technological landscape, we use large language models to dive deep and uncover the lesser-known stories that add depth and texture to our understanding of the world around us.

How It Works...

At the heart of The Dark Times is a large language model, which is a sophisticated AI that mimicks human-like language. By combining this technology with a search engine API, the AI quickly and efficiently gathers new information on a given topic and generates unique and interesting articles!

The Future...

Our goal is to do more than just recycle information; By staying at the cutting edge of technology, we want to generate genuinely thought-provoking takes on interesting topics, and deliver some of the most engaging content available on the internet.
